Welcome to DevenSMP™

A Unique Minecraft Survival Experience

DevenSMP™ is a community-driven Minecraft survival server featuring a dynamic economy, quests, and immersive gameplay. Established on 25/10/22, we started as a small group and evolved into an ambitious multiplayer experience.

How We Started

DevenSMP™ was originally created for a small group of friends to enjoy Minecraft together. Over time, it expanded into a structured server with its own unique mechanics and growing community.

What Makes DevenSMP™ Unique?

  1. Advanced survival mechanics (supply & demand economy, quests, fast travel, and banking)
  2. Tryhard & competitive players with casual elements
  3. Seasons-based resets after unrepairable major wars or inactivity
  4. A Live Map that shows player and loaded chunks in our server
  5. English as the main language
